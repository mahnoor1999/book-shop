Book Lovers - Welcome to your spiritual home

-More then just a bookstore. A place to discover and talk about books. Book lovers - welcome to your spiritual homeIn this beautiful, airy store, a wide range of fiction, new and used non-fiction and children’s titles throughout the store making the likelihood of you finding what you need. We are in the process of uploading all of titles to be online order.

We believe in offering the highest possible level of customer service a best place to indulge in a great book. That's partly about being able to order most in-print titles the next day or being like a dog-with-a-bone when tracking down for you that childhood book that you remember was blue and had a windmill on the cover. It's more than that though. It's running a shop that feels likes it's yours as well as ours. It's about instinctively knowing what books our regular customers will enjoy. It's about offering a memorable (if sometimes eccentric) experience to all visitors and about sharing and spreading our joyous book obsession with each of you

-Why stop at books? There is also a wonderful gift and clothing selection. Most gifts are suitable for all year round, but we always have some appropriate for each season. It’s easy to spend half an hour or even an hour in this section. We are proud to be part of this vibrant local community, and to welcome both visitors and locals alike.
